Program

Plumbers conference consists of two plenary opening and closing keynotes,  with the rest of the time being divided amongst three concurrent tracks: One track of traditional presentations and two tracks of MicroConferences.

The format will be 2.5 hours per MC.  Virtualization and Tracing are double wide in that they have two MC slots each.  Talks will be 45 minutes.

Additionally, we have Birds of a Feather sessions which will be scheduled around the main conference programme on a first come first served basis.
